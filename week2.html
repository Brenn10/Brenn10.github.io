Week 2

Goals

The goals for week 2 were to be able to use blob detection, visual servo toward a blob, and recognize different colored blobs.


Blobs are basically colored swatches. The words "contour" and "blob" both mean colored patches. We used pieces of paper for the blobs. Visual servoing is following a blob toward a target.
